Register Your Hub

Use this form after completing the Register in Directory checklist in your hub admin console (HUB tab). We review listing requests manually, add approved hubs to directory/hubs.json, and you refresh trust from the console with CHECK DIRECTORY.

Fastest path: open your hub → HUB → fill identity → GENERATE KEY → CHECK REACHABILITY → OPEN REGISTRATION FORM. Fields below pre-fill from that link.
Directory Guide

After you submit

  1. We verify your public URL and contact email.
  2. Your hub is added to the directory feed (usually listed first, verified after review).
  3. In your hub console, click CHECK DIRECTORY to pull trust metadata.
  4. Listed hubs appear in the SignalForge mobile app directory picker.
