Join the Hosted Hub

Create a free listener account on the main SignalForge hub at p7hub.projectseven.us. No invite code — enter your email, verify a 6-digit code, and you're in. Same account works in the web console and SignalForgeHub mobile app.

Create account on hub Get the mobile app

How it works

  1. 1
    Open the hub account screen. Click the button above — it opens the hosted hub directly on the sign-up form (?view=account).
  2. 2
    Enter your email and request a code. If this is your first time, the hub creates your account automatically when you submit your email. You do not need an existing login.
  3. 3
    Check your inbox. Enter the 6-digit code on the hub screen, or tap the link in the email. Codes expire after about 15 minutes — request a fresh one if needed.
  4. 4
    Listen live. Open Radio Sets in the console to hear live traffic, or sign in on mobile with the same email at https://p7hub.projectseven.us.

Running your own hub? Register your cell in the public directory for mobile discovery — separate from creating a listener account here.
